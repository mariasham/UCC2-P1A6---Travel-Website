Your Itinerary

This is where everything stops feeling scattered and starts making sense — day by day, at your pace.

Day 1 — Arrival

Land, get settled, and take your time adjusting. No pressure — just get comfortable.

Day 2 — Explore

Walk around, find local spots, and let yourself get a feel for the city.

Day 3 — Experiences

Plan something memorable — a tour, activity, or something you wouldn’t normally do.

Day 4 — Slow Day

Leave space to breathe. These moments usually end up meaning the most.

Day 5 — Wrap Up

Revisit your favorite place, take it in, and end the trip intentionally.

Tips for Planning

Don’t Overbook

Leave space for things you didn’t plan.

Group by Area

Save time by exploring nearby places together.

Stay Flexible

The best moments usually aren’t scheduled.

Itinerary

This is where your trip actually comes together. Instead of everything living in your head, you can lay it out day by day so it feels clear and easy to follow.

Day 1 — Arrival

Land, get settled, and take your time adjusting. No pressure to rush into anything — just get comfortable.

Day 2 — Explore the City

Walk around, find local spots, and let yourself get a feel for the place. This is where the trip starts to feel real.

Day 3 — Experiences

Plan something memorable — a tour, activity, or something you wouldn’t normally do at home.

Day 4 — Slow Day

Leave space to breathe. A slower day usually ends up being one of the best parts of a trip.

Day 5 — Wrap Up

Revisit a favorite place, grab something you didn’t get before, and take it all in before leaving.

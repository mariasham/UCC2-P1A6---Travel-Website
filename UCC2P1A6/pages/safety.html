Travel Safety

Feeling safe changes everything. When you know what to look for, you move differently — more aware, but still present.

Before You Go

Research Your Destination

Know the areas to avoid and what’s normal locally.

Save Important Info

Keep copies of your passport, bookings, and contacts.

Plan Your Arrival

Know how you're getting from the airport before you land.

While You’re There

Stay Aware

Pay attention to your surroundings without being paranoid.

Keep Valuables Close

Use a crossbody bag or keep essentials secure.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off, it probably is — listen to that.

Extra Precautions

Share Your Location

Let someone back home know where you are.

Use Trusted Transport

Stick to official taxis, rideshare apps, or hotel services.

Emergency Contacts

Know local emergency numbers and your embassy location.

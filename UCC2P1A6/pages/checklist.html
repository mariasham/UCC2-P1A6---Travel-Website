Travel Checklist

This is for the small things people forget when they’re excited to leave. Having everything ready makes the trip feel smoother and way less stressful.

Before You Leave

  • Passport or ID
  • Flight confirmation
  • Hotel reservation
  • Travel insurance (if needed)

Packing Essentials

  • Clothes based on weather
  • Toiletries
  • Chargers and electronics
  • Comfortable shoes

Extras

  • Snacks
  • Headphones
  • Portable charger
  • Backup cash or card
